About Us

Greetings from Pakhtoon, where each cup narrates a tale. Our quest started with a straightforward concept: to provide an environment where innovation and tradition may live together. Grounded in Peshawar's rich cultural legacy, our goal is to deliver an experience rather than merely a coffee shop.

Serving more than simply coffee is our goal. We are here to offer a taste of tradition, a slice of history, and a drink of real hospitality. Every item on our menu, from our carefully prepared drinks to our carefully chosen selection of regional specialties, pays homage to this city's colourful fabric.

Pakhtoon Cafe offers more than simply a place to satisfy your palate when you walk in. A portion of Peshawar's heart is yours to find.
